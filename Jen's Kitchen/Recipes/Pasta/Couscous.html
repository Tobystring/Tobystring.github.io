Couscous

Here's a simple recipe for making basic couscous:
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup couscous
  • 1 1/4 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter
  • Salt to taste
Instructions:
  1. In a saucepan, bring the water or vegetable broth to a boil.
  2. Stir in the olive oil or butter and a pinch of salt.
  3. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the couscous.
  4. Cover the saucepan with a lid and let it sit for about 5 minutes to allow the couscous to absorb the liquid.
  5. Fluff the couscous with a fork to separate the grains.
  6. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed with more salt or a drizzle of olive oil.
  7. Serve the couscous as a side dish with your favorite main course or mix in some roasted vegetables, herbs, nuts, or dried fruits for added flavor.
This basic couscous recipe is quick and easy to make, and you can customize it with additional ingredients to suit your taste preferences.
Enjoy your homemade couscous!
