Tapenade

Here's a classic recipe for olive tapenade:
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup pitted black olives
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 2 anchovy fillets (optional)
  • 1-2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. In a food processor, combine the black olives, capers, anchovy fillets, garlic cloves, and lemon juice. Pulse until the ingredients are finely chopped.
  2. Slowly drizzle in the olive oil while the food processor is running until the mixture reaches your desired consistency.
  3. Transfer the tapenade to a bowl and stir in the chopped parsley. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cover the bowl and refrigerate the tapenade for at least an hour to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve the tapenade with crackers, bread, or use it as a spread on sandwiches or as a topping for grilled fish or chicken.
Enjoy your homemade olive tapenade!
